Best movies according to Me.

My Top 3 all time favs.

A Simple Favor

Not PG13.
A Simple Favor is a fun and fashionable thriller accompanied by killer performances from Anna Kendrick and Blake Lively.Stephanie, a single mother, along with Sean, Emily's husband, set out to unravel the mystery behind Emily's disappearance. However, they counter secrets much more than what they bargained for.

Release date: September 14, 2018 (USA)

The Imitaition Game

A must-watch for history lovers.
British mathematician Alan Turing joins the cryptography team to help decrypt the Enigma code but builds his own machine that is a prototype of the modern computer to decipher German codes.

Release date: January 8, 2015 (United Arab Emirates)

The Dead Poets Society

A great movie for students and anyone who lives for poetry.
A group of students in a highly conservative boarding school learn to rebel against the status quo and find deeper meaning in life with the help of their new poetry teacher.

Release date: June 2, 1989 (USA)
